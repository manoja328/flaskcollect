Frequently Asked Questions?

This website is intended to collect questions and ansers for Visual Question Answering.
Users will be shown images and asked to input both question and answer relevant to the iamges. Pressing
the next button will show random images from our database. Users also have option to upload their own images
using the ChooseFile button to select the image and pressing upload to push it to our database.

We aim to collect Couting Questions ( starts with How many) i.e questions which have numerical answers.
Specifically, we encourage to ask hard questions which we call complex questions but simple questions are also nonethelsss valuable.

• What are simple questions?

For eg. How may people are there? Ans:2

How may dogs/people/cats/... are there ? Ans:2


• What are complex questions?

These question are not just demanding simple count but with added attributes like color , position etc.

For eg. How may people are left of the person ? Ans:1

How many blue cats are there ? Ans:1
